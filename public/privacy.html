Effective 10 May, 2020

VoluntYOU is a platform that brings volunteers, NGOs, and schools together, making the volunteering process easy and accessible to all. When you use our services, you'll be required to share some of your personal information with us. We believe your data privacy matters - that's why we've written our privacy policy free of the legal jargon that often fills these documents. Of course, if you still have questions please feel free to contact us at info@voluntyou.org.

Information we collect

VoluntYOU only collects the information you enter into the application. If you are a volunteer, this includes:

  • First and last name
  • Age
  • Volunteering events attended
  • Volunteering hours earned
  • Skills and interests
  • Email address
  • School attended

If you are a CSO, this includes:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Business phone number
  • Business email
  • Description of your organization and its goals
  • Planned events

If you are an academic institution, this includes but is not limited to:

  • School name
  • Location
  • School business email

How we use your information

We use the Firebase service from Google LLC. (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA) as a backed storage service and to derive application analytics. We use that information to see how users interact with our app.

Firebase is part of the Google Cloud Platform and offers numerous services for developers. A list can be found on this page. Some Firebase services process personal data. This data does not represent personally identifiable information for us. This aggregated data is used to analyze and optimize our users experience.

Currently, we use the following Firebase services:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Google Analytics provides analytics and attribution information. You can find more information via this link. and on Google’s partner policy. Google Analytics retains ID-associated data for 60 days and retains aggregate reporting and campaign data without automatic renewal unless the Firebase customer changes their retention preference in their Analytics settings, Google uses not only the “Instance ID” described but also the advertising ID of the end device. You can restrict the use of the advertising ID in the device settings of your mobile device. For iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > No ad tracking. VoluntYOU is an ad-free platform so this advertising ID remains unsued when you subscribe to our services.
  • Firebase Database: Firebase Database is a hosting and backend service provided by Google Inc: it stores the data VoluntYOU collects. This data is processed in the United States. For more information please visit their Privacy Policy.

For information on how Google complies with international privacy law, please visit this link.
